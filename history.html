Our History

Over 30 Years of Professional Excellence

Inherited Aptitude for Professionalism and Responsible Leadership

The principals of Sac Valley Electric, Brad Kirk and Doug Wirtz, were first inspired by their fathers who were also electricians. Both entered the trade at age 18, learning the industry literally from the ground up.

Working hard on a wide range of projects—from sports arenas to the restoration of historic buildings—they learned how to manage large, complex jobs and workforces, the necessity of teamwork, and the importance of understanding their client's needs and expectations.

Founding & Growth

In 2004, Brad and Doug started their own company, Sac Valley Electric, where they continued to apply their extensive knowledge and experience, offering creative and safe solutions to some of the most complex electrical challenges.

From the beginning, the company was built on the foundation of their values: dedication, diligence, and fine workmanship. These principles attracted talented electricians and project managers who shared their commitment to excellence.

Today

Sac Valley Electric is now a multi-million-dollar company handling a myriad of projects at:

  • Utility and public works sites and bridges
  • Technology and manufacturing companies
  • Government buildings and schools
  • Aerospace facilities
  • Fashionable restaurants and contemporary wine bars

Brad and Doug are extremely well respected in their field. Setting an example of dedication, diligence and fine workmanship, they have earned the admiration of their peers and the loyalty of their clients.

Union Commitment

Sac Valley Electric is a union shop committed to the highest standards of the electrical contracting industry. We exclusively hire and work with electricians from IBEW Local 340, ensuring that every project benefits from highly trained, skilled tradespeople who meet rigorous standards of safety, quality, and professionalism.

Our membership and leadership in NECA Sacramento underscores our commitment to advancing the electrical contracting profession and maintaining industry excellence.

Why We Do This

We believe that electrical excellence is about more than just technical skill. It's about understanding our clients' vision, delivering on our promises, and building relationships that last. Every project—whether it's a utility site, an aerospace facility, or a historic restoration—receives the same level of expertise and attention to detail.

That's what 30 years of professional service means. That's what Sac Valley Electric stands for.
