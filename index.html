Our Story

Inherited Aptitude for Professionalism and Responsible Leadership

The principals of Sac Valley Electric, Brad Kirk & Doug Wirtz, were first inspired by their fathers who were also electricians. Thus, both entered the trade at age 18, learning the industry literally from the ground up.

Working hard on a wide range of projects, from sports arenas to the restoration of historic buildings, they learned how to manage large complex jobs and workforces, the necessity of teamwork, and the importance of understanding their client's needs and expectations.

In 2004, Brad and Doug started their own company, Sac Valley Electric, where they continued to apply their extensive knowledge and experience, offering creative and safe solutions to some of the most complex electrical challenges.

Today, Sac Valley Electric is a multi-million-dollar company handling a myriad of projects at places such as utility and public works sites and bridges, technology and manufacturing companies, government buildings and schools, aerospace facilities, as well as fashionable restaurants and contemporary wine-bars.

Brad and Doug are extremely well respected in their field. Setting an example of dedication, diligence and fine workmanship, they have earned the admiration of their peers and the loyalty of their clients.
