About Sac Valley Electric

Sac Valley Electric is a multi-million-dollar commercial and industrial electrical contracting firm with over 30 years of experience serving Northern California. We specialize in large, complex projects for utility companies, government agencies, manufacturing facilities, aerospace companies, and major commercial clients.

Founded in 2004 by Brad Kirk and Doug Wirtz, the company is built on a foundation of expertise, integrity, and exceptional workmanship. We're a proud member of NECA Sacramento, partnering with IBEW Local 340 to deliver the highest standards of professional electrical contracting.

NECA Member - Industry Leadership

Sac Valley Electric is a member of NECA Sacramento, the association dedicated to excellence in electrical construction.

We partner with IBEW Local 340 union electricians, ensuring every project benefits from the industry's highest standards of safety, quality, and professionalism.

Brad Kirk serves as Governor of NECA Sacramento, advancing industry standards and professional excellence.
