About Sac Valley Electric

Sac Valley Electric is a multi-million-dollar commercial and industrial electrical contracting firm with over 30 years of experience serving Northern California. We specialize in large, complex projects for utility companies, government agencies, manufacturing facilities, aerospace companies, and major commercial clients.

Founded in 2004 by Brad Kirk and Doug Wirtz, the company is built on a foundation of expertise, integrity, and exceptional workmanship. We're known for delivering reliable, innovative solutions to complex electrical challenges.
